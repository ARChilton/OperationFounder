What is Event Hike Manager?

Event Hike Manager was developed initially for the Scout and Guide communities to enable real time updates on where the teams of young people are as they participate in orienteering activities.

Event Hike Manager can be used via the app or website to record when, where and who visited a specific location/base. With the added ability to record any scores taken at that location.

Replacing the need for radio updates or sending messangers to collect records of team sightings from accross the countryside.

What if I have bad mobile reception?

Being out in the countyside means there is no guarentee of mobile reception. While this situation is improving, Event Hike Manager has taken an 'offline first' approach.

Offline first means that this app/webapp will continue to work even when your mobile internet signal is intermittent. It does this by storing the recorded base logs to your mobile device before synchronising them with other event users when the mobile device re-connects to the internet.
